From Mech to Tech

From an early age I have enjoyed tinkering. Successfully putting something back together after you have taken it apart is an extremely satisfying feeling. After many years of putting this passion for problem solving towards motorcycles, cars, and bicycles, I decided to turn my focus to the infinitely more complex discipline of website development and blockchain technologies. In 2017 I enrolled in a Full Stack Web Development Certification program at the University of Minnesota, where not only did I start to really care about what makes a web application tick, I also wanted to learn how to make such an application both beautiful, and efficient. Learning code in the certification program quickly led to me becoming increasingly interested in the technology of blockchains, and all the ways they could potentially change the way many businesses operate altogether.
